Privacy Policy for Dia Block

Last Updated:

This Privacy Policy describes how Dia Block ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Game Data

To provide you with a personalized gaming experience, we collect:

  • Game Progress: Your current level, completed levels, and game achievements
  • Game Scores: Your scores for each level and global score
  • Username: A randomly generated username for leaderboard display (e.g., "CoolPlayer1234")
  • Game Statistics: Number of moves, completion times, and other gameplay metrics

1.2 Authentication Data

We use Firebase Authentication to provide anonymous authentication. This creates a unique user ID that is not linked to your personal identity.

1.3 Device Information

We may collect certain device information automatically, including:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version
  • Advertising ID (for ad personalization)

1.4 Advertising Data

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information for ad targeting
  • Interaction data with advertisements

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

1.5 Purchase Information

If you make in-app purchases (subscriptions), Google Play processes your payment. We receive confirmation of your purchase but do not store your payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Save and sync your game progress across devices
  • Display leaderboards and rankings
  • Provide personalized gaming experiences
  • Show relevant advertisements
  • Process in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Improve our App and fix bugs
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance gameplay

3. Data Storage and Security

Your game data is stored securely using:

  • Firebase Cloud Firestore: For cloud saves and leaderboards
  • Local Storage: As a backup on your device

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication, cloud storage, and analytics. Firebase Privacy
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. AdMob Privacy
  • Google Play Billing: For processing in-app purchases. Google Privacy Policy

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

5. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your game data stored in our systems
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of personalized advertising through your device settings
  • Cancel subscriptions through Google Play Store settings

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

7. Data Retention

We retain your game data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you delete the App, your data may be retained for a reasonable period for backup and recovery purposes.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
